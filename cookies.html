What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit websites. Cookies enable websites to recognise visitors, remember preferences, and collect information about how websites are used. Cookies are not programs and cannot damage your device or access your files. They simply contain information about your browsing activity and preferences.

How We Use Cookies

Web Design Specialists Ltd. uses cookies for the following purposes:

Analytics Cookies

We use analytics cookies to understand how visitors use our website. These cookies collect information about page views, visitor duration, referral sources, and browsing paths. This data helps us improve website performance, user experience, and content relevance. Analytics data is aggregated and anonymised, meaning individual visitors cannot be personally identified. We use Google Analytics for this purpose. Google processes analytics data in accordance with their privacy policy.

Functional Cookies

These cookies enable website functionality and remember your preferences. Examples include language selection, previously visited pages, and form data persistence. Functional cookies enhance your experience by allowing the website to remember your choices across visits.

Tracking Cookies

We may use tracking pixels or cookies to measure website visitor behaviour and understand which pages are most valuable. This allows us to continually improve website design and user experience. Third-party advertising platforms may also place cookies on your device if you arrive from an advertisement.

Types of Cookies

Essential Cookies

These cookies are strictly necessary for website operation and cannot be disabled. They enable core functionality such as security features, form validation, and navigation. These cookies do not require user consent.

Performance Cookies

Performance cookies collect information about how you use our website, including pages visited, time spent, and navigation paths. This data helps us optimise website performance and user experience. These cookies require your consent.

Preference Cookies

Preference cookies remember your choices and settings, such as language preferences and site customisations. These cookies enhance user experience by personalising the website to your preferences. These cookies require your consent.

Marketing Cookies

Marketing cookies track your activity across websites and collect information about your interests. This data is used by advertising networks to serve targeted advertisements. These cookies require your explicit consent.

Third-Party Cookies

Third-party service providers may place cookies on your device when you visit our website. These include analytics providers, advertising networks, and social media platforms. Third-party cookies enable features such as sharing content on social platforms and viewing embedded content. You can control third-party cookies through your browser settings or by opting out of specific services.

Cookie Consent and Management

When you first visit our website, you will be presented with a cookie consent notice. You can choose to accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or configure specific cookie categories. Your consent choices are saved in a cookie so we remember your preferences on future visits.

Changing Your Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by clearing your browser cookies and revisiting our website, or by using your browser's cookie management settings. You can also opt out of analytics cookies through Google Analytics' opt-out browser extension.

Browser Controls

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or alert you when cookies are being set. However, disabling cookies may affect website functionality and your user experience. Specific instructions for managing cookies in popular browsers are available through browser help features.

Cookie Duration

Cookies have varying lifespans. Session cookies are deleted when you close your browser. Persistent cookies remain on your device for a specified period (typically days, months, or years) and continue to operate across multiple visits. You can view and delete cookies through your browser settings at any time.

UK Regulatory Compliance

Cookies that are not strictly necessary for website operation require prior user consent. We provide clear cookie consent mechanisms and allow users to manage their preferences. All cookie usage is documented in this policy, and we maintain transparency about what data is collected and how it is used.
