Services and Projects

What services does Web Design Specialists Ltd. provide?

We provide professional web design, development, user experience optimisation, e-commerce solutions, and website maintenance. Please see our Services page for detailed information about each offering.

Do you build e-commerce websites?

Yes, we specialise in creating e-commerce platforms optimised for customer acquisition and conversion. We work with platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce, or develop custom e-commerce solutions depending on your requirements and scale.

Can you redesign my existing website?

Yes, website redesign is a core service. We analyse your existing website, identify improvement opportunities, and develop modernised designs that maintain your brand identity while improving functionality and user experience.

How long does a website project typically take?

Project timelines vary significantly based on complexity, scope, and content availability. Simple websites may require 4-8 weeks, while complex e-commerce platforms or custom applications may require 12-20 weeks or more. We provide detailed project timelines in our proposal.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

Absolutely. All websites we develop are fully responsive and mobile-optimised. They function seamlessly across all device types and screen sizes, from large desktop monitors to mobile phones.

Website Functionality and Features

Can you add a blog or news section to my website?

Yes, we can incorporate content management systems (CMS) like WordPress that enable you to easily create, edit, and publish blog posts and news articles independently without technical knowledge.

Will my website rank in Google?

We incorporate search engine optimisation (SEO) best practices throughout website design and development, providing strong technical foundations for search visibility. However, search rankings also depend on content quality, link building, and ongoing SEO efforts. We recommend discussing SEO strategy separately from design and development.

Can you integrate third-party services with my website?

Yes, we regularly integrate third-party services including payment processors, email marketing platforms, CRM systems, analytics tools, and other services. Integration possibilities depend on API availability and complexity of the integration.

Privacy and Security

How do you protect my personal data and project information?

We implement comprehensive data protection measures including encryption, access controls, and regular security audits. All data handling complies with UK GDPR requirements. Please see our Privacy Policy for detailed information.

Will my website be secure?

Yes, we implement security best practices throughout website development including SSL/TLS encryption, secure authentication, input validation, and protection against common web vulnerabilities. We recommend ongoing security monitoring and updates after launch.

How do I protect customer payment data?

For e-commerce sites, we implement PCI DSS-compliant payment processing ensuring customer card data is never stored on your servers. We use trusted payment gateways that handle sensitive data securely. We also advise clients on security best practices for customer data.

Billing and Project Management

How do you structure your pricing?

Pricing varies depending on project scope, complexity, and custom requirements. We provide detailed proposals outlining scope, deliverables, timeline, and investment. We offer various engagement models including project-based, time-and-materials, and retainer arrangements.

What happens after my website launches?

After launch, we provide post-launch support addressing any issues. We offer maintenance packages including security updates, software updates, performance monitoring, and ongoing support. Ongoing maintenance is important for website security and performance.

Can I make changes to my website after it launches?

If your website uses a content management system (CMS), you can make many changes independently. We also provide maintenance services for changes or updates. We recommend reviewing your website regularly and planning periodic updates to keep content fresh and technology current.
